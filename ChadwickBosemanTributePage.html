Chadwick Aaron Boseman

The Man who changed the world twice: daily through his living work; once again in his afterlife.

Most known for his 2018 role as the character T'Challa in the film Black Panther, Boseman was an aspring example of Black excellence and resilience in the movie industry.

From his humble beginnings of Anderson, South Carolina
To his final days in Los Angeles, California. Chadwick was an inspiration to all around him
